Smart Trend Detection

The bot filters short-term market noise. EMA 9/21 crosses are tracked for context but never trigger standalone alerts.

Alerts are generated only when:

• Medium-term trends shift (EMA 21 × 50)

• Long-term trends reverse (EMA 50 × 200)

• Market structure changes (classification shift)

Not every crossover matters. Only the ones that change the trend.

Alert Priority

CRITICAL
EMA 50 × EMA 200
Golden cross / Death cross. Long-term trend reversal.
HIGH
EMA 21 × EMA 50
Medium-term trend shift. Primary alert source.
MEDIUM
Classification Change
Market structure shifts between 6 states.
LOW
Price / RSI / Volume / BB
Indicator alerts. Suppressed when trend alerts fire.
CONTEXT
EMA 9 × EMA 21
Short-term momentum. Never triggers alerts.
